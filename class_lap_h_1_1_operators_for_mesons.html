Detailed Description

Calculates operators as they emerge in correlation functions using the stochastic estimates from the stochastic Laplacian Heaviside method

Basically this calculates every operator of the form

  • VdaggerV : \( V^\dagger exp(ipx) V \)
  • rVdaggerV : \( (P^(b)\rho V)^\dagger exp(ipx) V \)
  • rVdaggerVr : \( (P^(b)\rho V)^\dagger exp(ipx) (P^(b)\rho V) \)

It is straightforward to generalize the operators for Displacement, but not implemented

Implement derivate operators

Note
(MW 3.12.2017) rVdaggerVr moved to QuarkLineBlock
